Cloud Computing & Migration

Cloud Computing refers to the delivery of computing services—such as servers, storage, databases, networking, software, and analytics—over the internet (“the cloud”). It enables businesses to access these resources on-demand, scale operations efficiently, and reduce costs by eliminating the need for on-premise infrastructure.
